How to play ?

Welcome to Quizzzy Quizz an exciting computer science challenge! This is a perfect game to test your knowledge in computer science. You will be asked 10 questions from various computer science categories. Each correct answer earns you 1 point,and the maximum score you can get is 10. The questions are chosen form topics like algorithms, programming languages, and web development. Your journey begins by selecting a category within BEGINNER, INTERMEDIATE AND VETERAN. Your final score will determine your grade. Get ready to embark on this intellectual adventure and see how well you fare in the realm of bits and bytes!
